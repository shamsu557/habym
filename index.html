Who We Are

Built for
Nigerian Trade.

HABYM Nigeria Ltd is a duly incorporated private limited liability company under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 (CAMA), registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Headquartered in Kano — Nigeria's premier commercial hub for Northern commodities — we operate as exporters, importers, distributors, wholesalers, and trade agents across a broad spectrum of goods and commodities.

Our operations span agricultural produce, cash crops, solid minerals, and industrial inputs, connecting Nigerian markets to domestic and international buyers with reliable logistics.

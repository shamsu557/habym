Who We Are

A New Era of Nigerian Commerce

"We connect Nigerian produce and products to domestic and international buyers — with integrity, efficiency, and purpose."

HABYM Nigeria Ltd is a duly incorporated private company under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 (CAMA), registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. We are a fully Nigerian-owned enterprise, headquartered in Kano.

Our core mandate is the export, import, distribution, and trading of all categories of goods — from agricultural commodities and cash crops to industrial inputs and general merchandise. We serve both domestic and international markets with competitive pricing and reliable supply chains.

Our Vision

To become a leading commodity trading company connecting Northern Nigeria to global markets.

Our Mission

Delivering quality goods with transparent trade practices and building lasting international partnerships.

What We Do

Core Services & Activities

From agricultural commodities to general merchandise, HABYM operates across a wide spectrum of trading activities — locally and internationally. Our operations are anchored in Kano and extend across Nigeria and beyond.
01
🌍

Export & Import Trading

We act as exporters, importers, and agents of all kinds of goods, wares, and commodities — whether produced locally or internationally. Full trade facilitation from procurement to delivery.

02
🌾

Agricultural Commodity Trading

Specialising in agricultural produce and cash crops including sesame seeds, groundnuts, soybeans, hibiscus, ginger, and other Northern Nigerian commodities for domestic and export markets.

03
⛏️

Solid Minerals Supply

Trading in solid minerals and industrial raw materials, connecting Nigerian mineral resources to domestic processors and international buyers through transparent and compliant channels.

04
🏭

Industrial Input Supply

Sourcing and distributing industrial inputs for manufacturing and processing sectors across Nigeria, ensuring steady supply chains for businesses that depend on consistent material flows.

05
📦

Wholesale & Distribution

Operating as wholesalers, retailers, and manufacturers' representatives, we distribute a broad range of products through established networks across key Nigerian markets.

06
🤝

Trade Agency & Representation

Serving as licensed agents and representatives for foreign suppliers and buyers seeking a reliable local partner in Nigeria with deep knowledge of the Northern Nigerian market.
